        Professional Summary

        Prior to employment with Colliers International Dustin was employed with Fremantle Property Services as a Commercial Leasing Agent from January 2013 July 2014.

        While at Fremantle Property Services Dustin gained significant experience and had outstanding success within the local commercial property market, completing in excess of 40% of the commercial leasing transactions occurring in Fremantle during his period of employment.

        Dustin also has extensive background knowledge in marketing and the residential property market, working as the Business Development Manager and Leasing Agent with boutique Fremantle firm Empire Property Solutions from June 2012 December 2013.

        Prior to employment with Empire Property Solutions, Dustin completed a Diploma of Commerce at Curtin University International College whilst working part time in Property Management with Primus Realty.


        Accomplishments

        Dustin commenced his employment with Colliers International in August 2014 as an Executive of Office Leasing.

        His passion for commercial property, drive to succeed and ability to execute has seen him excel in this role as demonstrated by his track record, successfully brokerng in excess of 100 leasing transactions with Colliers International (WA).

        Dustin has built relationships with owners and tenants alike in a range of markets, with a track record that extends across the Perth CBD, West Perth, CBD Fringe locations and Suburban Regions. The range of locations he has been able to succeed in gives him an extremely broad knowledge base that is unrivalled by competitors.

        Dustin leverages his knowledge of the Perth market, his existing skill base of negotiating commercial leases, sourcing and qualifying appropriate office occupants and property management to ensure a successful transaction is executed that adds value to the overall client service experience.
